ABOUT OUR COMPANY

30 years of expertise in kitchenware you can trust.

深耕廚房用品領域三十年，
品質值得信賴

本司於1995年成立，持續於廚房之玻璃、五金與各式用品領域深耕，並持續致力於北美與歐洲各地之出口，並可為客戶提供各式主力產品的打造，持續為客人提供新產品開發及生產。本司在中國大陸也設有分廠與辦公室，亦可提供多元的出貨方式及包裝，以提供最利於客戶之方式為之服務 。

Established in 1995 in Taiwan, Chnglass Enterprise specializes in kitchen glass, hardware, and related supplies, exporting to North America and Europe. We are committed to providing customers with diverse product offerings and ongoing new product development and manufacturing. With a branch office in mainland China, Chnglass Enterprise offers a variety of shipping and packaging options to ensure optimal customer service.
